EnglishRoad has two tools for English learners. One estimates your level. The other gives short practice quizzes with detailed feedback.

Tool 1

Level Check

Answer 100 questions. The app estimates your EnglishRoad level, CEFR, TOEFL, IELTS, and TOEIC ranges. It also shows strong areas and practice areas.

  • Good for placement conversations
  • Uses a balanced sample from 2,200 items
  • Creates an unofficial report after 100 answers
Start level check

Tool 2

Practice Quizzes

Choose A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, or C2. Take a 25-question quiz from that level and copy an AI study prompt for deeper explanations.

  • Good for study and review
  • No repeated questions in a 25-item quiz
  • AI prompt for each answer choice
